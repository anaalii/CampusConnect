Welcome to CampusConnect

The CampusConnect was developed to ease the college management for key-in and submitting data into the system. Before, the process of collecting data was still manually by Unit Residensi dan Hospitaliti Pelajar (URHP). The organization that handles UiTM Kota Kinabalu college is Unit Residensi dan Hospitaliti Pelajar (URHP) together with Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej (JPK) and other departments that are involved with facilities and campus surroundings.
