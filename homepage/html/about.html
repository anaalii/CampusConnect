About Us

Welcome to CampusConnect

Prior to the implementation of CampusConnect, the process of managing UiTM Kota Kinabalu's student housing data was manual and time-consuming. This involved URHP staff manually collecting data from various sources, such as student applications, housing assignments, and maintenance records. This manual process was prone to errors and delays, and it made it difficult to track the status of student housing applications and maintenance requests.

The CampusConnect system was developed to address these challenges and streamline the management of student housing data. The system is a web-based application that allows URHP staff to easily key in and submit data related to student housing. The system also automates many of the tasks that were previously done manually, such as generating housing assignments and tracking the status of maintenance requests.
